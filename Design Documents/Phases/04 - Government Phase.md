# 3. Government Phase (§7.3)

The third New Year phase. In Turn Order, players take turns playing cards to
improve their position — through **Diplomacy**, **Industry**, **Technology**,
and **Intelligence** — then Diplomacy is resolved and hands are trimmed to the
HandSize Limit.

## Sequence

1. **Government Cardplay** in Turn Order (§7.31–7.35), continuing until **three
   consecutive Passes** end cardplay.
2. **Diplomacy Resolution** (§7.4)
3. **HandSize Compliance** (§7.5)

Cards have multiple possible uses, so a card must be played **upright from the
playing player's point of view** to show the intended purpose.

> **Onus to play:** the player with the largest "Overhand" (most cards over
> HandSize) is pressured — smaller Overhands can Pass and force the largest
> Overhand to either play first or lose all Overhand cards when cardplay ends.

---

## Cardplay options

Each turn, the Active Player chooses **one** of the following (or Passes):

### 7.31 Diplomacy (1 card)
Play **one Action card** with the target Neutral shown upright to gain Influence
over that Neutral. If a Rival has a Diplomacy card in play for that same Neutral,
**discard both** (opposing diplomacy cancels). See Diplomacy (§8.0).

### 7.32 Industry (several cards)
Play Investment cards whose Factory values total **at least** your current
**Factory Cost** (sidebar) to raise your **IND** level by one (adjust the marker
immediately).

> **Important:** IND cannot be raised more than **twice per Year**.

### 7.33 Technology (2 cards)
Play **two Investment cards** with the same desired Technology to Achieve it:

- Played **upright** (from your point of view) = revealed.
- Played **face-up** to Achieve immediately, discarding one of the pair (your
  choice) and keeping the other in front of you.
- Or place the Tech-pair **face-down** in your **Secret Vault** (§9.3) to Reveal
  later at a crucial moment.

Each Tech-pair in your Secret Vault reduces your HandSize Limit by one.
See Technology (§9.0).

### 7.34 Intelligence (1 card)
Some Investment cards have an **Intelligence (Intel)** function. When one is
played, **immediately execute** its instructions (§9.5). Examples: Agent, Code
Break, Spy Ring, Coup, Sabotage, Mole, Double Agent.

### 7.35 Pass (no cards)
Play no card. When **three players Pass in succession**, Government cardplay
ends and Diplomacy is resolved (§7.4).

---

## 7.4 Diplomacy Resolution

Resolve Diplomacy in **Turn Order**. For each Diplomacy card remaining in play,
place or remove an **Influence marker** as per §8.0 (opposing cards and markers
cancel each other).

Immediately **adjust POPulation and RESource levels** for any territory/control
changes that result.

## 7.5 HandSize Compliance

All players must now comply with their **HandSize Limit** (per their Production
track), **discarding** if necessary.

- Standard limits: **Axis 7 / West 8 / USSR 6**.
- Remember each **Tech pair** in your **Secret Vault** (§9.3) counts as one
  against your allowed HandSize Limit.

---

## Two-player note (§18.2)

A **Passive Ally** has constrained Government cardplay and can only play cards
from its hand (not from its face-down Passive Stack). World Reaction cards
(§15.41) go directly into the Passive Ally's hand and can be played thereafter.
